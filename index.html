Know when Dutch house prices actually change.

We track listing prices for rentals and homes for sale across Amsterdam and Haarlem — expanding across the Randstad. See which listings are genuinely new, which have been quietly relisted, and which prices are on their way down.

  • Full price history — see every price change on a listing from day one, not just what it's listed at today.
  • Relist detection — spot listings that were taken off the market and put back, sometimes at a "new" price.
  • Daily tracking — we check listings every day so you know the moment a price drops or a new listing goes live.
